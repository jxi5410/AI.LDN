Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 March 2026

What LDN/ai Is

LDN/ai (londonai.network) is a free, open intelligence platform mapping London's AI ecosystem. It is built and maintained by Jie Xi as a personal project.

What Data We Collect

Anonymous visitors: We use Vercel Analytics for basic, privacy-friendly traffic analytics (page views, referrers). No cookies are set for anonymous visitors. No personal data is collected. No advertising trackers are present.

Registered users: If you create an account (optional), we store your email address and any profile information you provide via Supabase Auth. This data is stored in EU-West-2 (London) region servers. You can delete your account at any time by contacting us.

API users: The public API does not require authentication and does not log personal data. The sourcing API (beta) requires a key but does not collect personal data beyond the key itself.

Company Data

All company data displayed on LDN/ai is sourced from public records: Companies House filings, public funding announcements, career pages, GitHub repositories, and news articles. We do not collect or display private company data. If you represent a company and wish to update or remove your listing, please contact us.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties. We do not display advertisements. We do not use tracking pixels or retargeting.

Cookies

LDN/ai does not set cookies for anonymous visitors. If you create an account, a session cookie is used for authentication only.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete any personal data we hold. To exercise these rights, contact: jxi5410@gmail.com.

AI-Generated Content

Sector analyses on LDN/ai are generated with the assistance of AI and are clearly labelled as such. The "Ask Deeper" feature uses the Anthropic API (Claude) to generate responses. Queries submitted to Ask Deeper are processed by Anthropic's API and are subject to their privacy policy.
